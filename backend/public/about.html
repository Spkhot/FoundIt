About FoundIt

The story behind our mission to reconnect people with their cherished belongings through the power of community and technology.

Our Mission

Every day, countless items—wallets, phones, keys, and irreplaceable memories—go missing. We saw a gap: a lack of a simple, trustworthy, and centralized system for people to report what they've found or search for what they've lost.

That's why we created FoundIt. Our mission is to be the definitive bridge between a lost item and its owner, fostering a global community built on kindness, trust, and the simple desire to help one another.

The Principles That Guide Us

Community First

We believe in the collective power of people. Our platform is a tool to amplify the inherent kindness within our communities.

Radical Simplicity

The process of reporting or finding an item should be effortless. We are obsessed with creating a clear, fast, and intuitive user experience.

Privacy & Safety

Your security is non-negotiable. We ensure all interactions are anonymous and communications are encrypted until you decide otherwise.

Join Our Mission

Be part of a movement that spreads kindness, one found item at a time. Your next search or post could make someone's day.
